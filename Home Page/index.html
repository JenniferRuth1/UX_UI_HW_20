About

Hi I'm Jennifer! I'm a UX UI Designer in San Antonio, Texas

I am a UX/UI Designer who is curious by nature. I care about details, questioning, learning and growing in all aspects of my life. My years in management have equipped me with strong communication, and problem-solving skills. I believe in collaboration, knowing we bring out the best in each other when we are open.

When I am not designing I am outside in my large vegetable gardens, making pickles and jam from my homegrown produce, in the garage tinkering on my 1957 Studebaker farm truck, or hiking with my dog Murphy.

Skills:

Product Design, User Experience, Design Interaction, Design Mobile Design, Web Design, User Research Usability, Testing Personas, Scenarios Prototyping, HTML & CSS.

Tools:

Miro, Figma, Adobe XD, InVision, Trello, Google, Visual Studio Code, & GitHub.
